Text Types أنواع النصوص

IB Arabic B requires you to produce a variety of text types in Paper 1 (Written Tasks) and the Internal Assessment. Knowing the conventions of each type is essential for full marks.

Personal and Informal Texts النصوص الشخصية

Personal Letter / Email رسالة شخصية / بريد إلكتروني

  • Opens with a greeting: عزيزي / عزيزتي followed by the recipient's name
  • First paragraph introduces why you are writing
  • Body paragraphs develop the topic with personal language
  • Closes with a farewell phrase: مع تحياتي / أخوك المخلص
  • Signature at the bottom (your name or a fictional sender)

Blog Post / Diary Entry مدونة / يوميات

  • Diary: opens with a date and informal address (عزيزي يومياتي)
  • First-person voice throughout; emotional and reflective tone
  • Blog: catchy title, direct address to readers, use of questions
  • Informal vocabulary but correct grammar
  • Closing reflection or call to action for blogs

Formal and Public Texts النصوص الرسمية

Formal Letter / Complaint رسالة رسمية / شكوى

  • Sender and recipient details at the top right and left respectively
  • Date below the header
  • Opens with: السيد / السيدة المحترم/ة or إلى من يهمه الأمر
  • Formal register throughout – no contractions or colloquialisms
  • Clearly structured: reason for writing → details → request or action
  • Closes with: وتفضلوا بقبول فائق الاحترام والتقدير
SL Task 2HL Task 2

Article / Report مقال / تقرير

  • Bold headline that summarises the topic
  • Byline (by + author name) beneath the headline
  • Opening paragraph with the main point (inverted pyramid structure)
  • Subheadings divide sections for readability
  • Third-person or mixed perspective; factual and analytical tone
  • May include statistics, expert quotes, or examples
SL Task 2HL Task 2

Speech / Interview خطاب / مقابلة

  • Speech: opens by addressing the audience (أيها الحضور الكرام)
  • Rhetorical questions and repetition for effect
  • Strong conclusion with a call to action or memorable statement
  • Interview: alternating question (س:) and answer (ج:) format
  • Interviewer questions are brief; answers are developed and detailed
HL Task 2

Brochure / Poster كتيب / ملصق

  • Bold title at the top
  • Short, punchy sentences or bullet points
  • May include subheadings and short paragraphs
  • Address the reader directly (أنت / أنتم)
  • Include a contact detail, website, or call to action at the bottom
SL Task 1

Literary Text Types (HL only) النصوص الأدبية

Short Story / Narrative قصة قصيرة

  • Exposition: introduces setting, time, and characters
  • Rising action: develops tension or conflict
  • Climax: turning point of the story
  • Resolution: ending – open or closed
  • Use of descriptive language, dialogue, and Arabic literary devices
  • First or third person narration – be consistent
HL Only
Key rule: Every text type has a set of conventions (layout, register, opening, closing). The examiner awards marks specifically for following these conventions. Learn them by reading authentic examples in Arabic.
